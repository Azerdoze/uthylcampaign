Beveritan General Traits

Name Description
Armor Restrictions Unless stated otherwise, beveritans can't use heavy armor.

Aevenai Traits

Name Description
Diplomatic +2 Diplomacy with Beveritan and Gernum Tribes. Begin play with Gernum extra language.
Wealthy Characters begin play with double the amount of starting gold.
Metalworkers +2 to profession and craft checks with metal items (armor/weapon/etc).
Civilized One extra initial language; +2 Diplomacy with civilized societies.
Heavy Spearmen +2 to Atk & Dmg rolls with weapons from the Spear group.
Reliable Boars Must choose Boar Animal Companion. Boars begin play with Advanced & Giant simple templates.

Buori Traits

Name Description
Raiders +2 to Intimidate checks; +2 to Atk & Dmg rolls with weapons from the Axe group.
Skilled Herders +4 to Profession (Herder) checks.
Bad Reputation -2 to Diplomacy checks with other Beveritan Tribes.
Naked Warriors +X to Initiative and to dodge AC, where X is CON MOD, if wearing no armor.
Horde Tactics Begin play with one extra Teamwork Feat.
Trained War Aurochs Must choose Auroch Animal Companion. Aurochs begin play with advanced simple template.
Illiterate Players must spend 2 skill points to be able to write and read.

Noeri Traits

Name Description
Fearless +8 to will saves versus fear effects.
Ambushers +2 to Move Silently & Hide checks; On surprise rounds they get +4 to Atk & DMG rolls.
Untiring Infantry Endurance as extra feat.
Horn Skalds. Skalds are Herald of the Horn Archetype; They Gain +1 spell/tier according to their CHA mod; +1 To spells ECL.
Horn Blowers +2 to all bonus effects of Raging Songs.
Horde Tactics Begin play with one extra Teamwork Feat.
Aescetic Lifestyle Can't be a salesmen and avoid ANY type of luxuries (alcohol, drugs, prostitutes, riches, etc).
Illiterate Players must spend 2 skill points to be able to write and read.

Bertian Traits

Name Description
Heavy Cavalry Can use Heavy Armor; +2 to Ride checks; +2 to ACP while riding; +2 to Atk & DMG with Mounted Melee Combat.
Skilled Farmers +2 to Profession (Farmer) checks.
Civilized One extra initial language; +2 Diplomacy with civilized societies.
Druidic Reverance Must Worship Druidism; Druids gain +1 spel/tier according to their WIS MOD.
Horde Tactics begin play with one extra Teamwork Feat.
Trained War Elks Must Choose Elk as Animal Companion. Elks have advanced and giant simple templates.
Chivalric Lifestyle Must be Lawful; Never refuse an honorable challenge; Always respect another.

Pieni Traits

Name Description
Ambushers +2 to Move Silently & Hide checks; On surprise rounds they get +4 to Atk & DMG rolls.
Fear Tactics +4 to rolls that provoke fear effects.
Powerful Infantry Charge +4 to Atk and damage rolls on charges. Must be on foot.
Head Hunters +2 Damage with Slashing Weapons; +4 to confirm critical hits with slashing weapons.
Naked Warriors +X to Iniative and dodge AC. Where X is CON MOD.
Painted Ones +2 to Atk rolls at Night.
Illiterate Players need to spend 2 skill points to able to write and read.

Keltirian Traits

Name Description
Good Neighbours +2 to Diplomacy checks with Gallitians
Ambushers +2 to Move Silently & Hide checks; On surprise rounds they get +4 to Atk & DMG rolls.
Skilled Herders +4 to Profession (Herder) checks.
Mountain Scouts Ignore Difficult terrain and get +2 to Survival checks in Mountain (and hills);
Dard Throwers +2 to Atk and +4 to DMG rolls with thrown Javelins.
Bull Warriors Must choose Cattle (Bull) as animal companion. Bulls have advanced simple template.
Illiterate Players need to spend 2 skill points to able to write and read.

Galitian Traits

Name Description
Mountain Warfare +2 to Initiative and dodge AC in Mountain (and Hills) terrain.
Good Infantry While on foot, get +2 to ATK rolls.
Isolated -2 to Diplomacy and Bluff checks.
Isolated Fighters +2 To ATK rolls and +2 to dodge AC while fighting alone. A companion doesn't count.
Trained War Rams Must choose Ram as Animal Companion; Rams have advanced simple template and +2 NAT armor.
Skilled Herders +4 to Profession (Herder) checks.
Worshippers of Bandua Characters must worship Bandua.
Illiterate Players need to spend 2 skill points to able to write and read.

Eastari Traits

Name Description
Desert Warfare +2 to INI rolls and +2 to Dodge AC in Desert (sand & wastelands) terrain.
Naked Warriors +X to Iniative and dodge AC. Where X is CON MOD.
Raiders +2 to Intimidate checks; +2 to Atk & Dmg rolls with weapons from the Axe group.
Lampa Affinity +2 to Diplomacy checks with Lamporia, Santea, Makeronia, Corkos, Tebea & Baciria.
Readers of Dunes +4 to Knowledge (Nature) Checks in Desert (Sand & Wastelands) terrain.
Horde Tactics begin play with one extra Teamwork Feat.
